Professional Profile

Called to the Ghana Bar in 2001, Mrs. Ruby Aglagoh has built a distinguished career spanning over two decades. She began her legal journey as a Pupil Associate at Awoonor Law Consultancy and subsequently served as In-House Counsel for Provident Insurance Company Limited.

She further honed her skills in corporate law and practice as a Senior Associate at JLD&MB Legal Consultancy and a Partner at Alloh & Partners, where she was involved in high-profile corporate transactions and advisory work. Ruby has extensive knowledge of corporate governance and is adept at providing advice on compliance with regulatory regimes gained through many years of experience as a Company Secretary, Director and Legal Adviser.
