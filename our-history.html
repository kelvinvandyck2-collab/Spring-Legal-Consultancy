About Spring Legal Consultancy

Spring Legal Consultancy (SLC) is a full-service corporate law firm that has evolved from a sole practitioner practice established in 2012 into a reputable partnership serving a broad and diverse clientele. We are guided by the highest standards of integrity, fairness, and professionalism, which define our approach to client service and legal practice.

Meet Our Partners

Founder/Partner

Mrs. Ruby Akua Aglagoh

Human Rights Advocate | Notary Public | Adjunct Lecturer

Called to the Ghana Bar in 2001, Mrs. Ruby Aglagoh has built a distinguished career spanning over two decades. She began her legal journey as a Pupil Associate at Awoonor Law Consultancy and subsequently served as In-House Counsel for Provident Insurance Company Limited.

Junior Partner

Ernest Assie, Esq.

Barrister & Solicitor

Mr Assie was called to the Ghana Bar in October 2017 and is a Junior Partner at Spring Legal Consultancy. He has 16 years of diverse prior experience in Recovery and Debt Management, Project Planning and Documentation.

Managing Partner

Alexander Nii Kwartey Owoo, Esq.

Barrister & Solicitor | Notary Public | Lecturer

Alexander Nii Kwartey Owoo is an accomplished legal professional with over two decades of experience in corporate law, banking, finance, trusts, family law and insurance.
